# Privacy Policy for FuzzyGuard

**Effective Date: May 14, 2026**

## 1. Introduction and Scope
Welcome to **FuzzyGuard**. This Privacy Policy explains our commitment to protecting your privacy and outlines our data processing practices. By installing, accessing, or using the FuzzyGuard application, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to be bound by the terms described in this policy. 

FuzzyGuard is engineered as an offline, local decision-support algorithm based on Fuzzy Logic (Mamdani - Centroid methodologies). Our primary architectural principle is "Privacy by Design."

## 2. Data Collection and Processing (Zero-Data Architecture)
FuzzyGuard requires certain physical and physiological parameters (e.g., age, Body Mass Index, cardiovascular metrics, and activity duration) to compute algorithmic risk indices. 

**We explicitly state the following regarding your data:**
* **No Cloud Storage:** All data entered into the App is processed strictly locally within your device's physical memory (RAM).
* **No Transmission:** We do not collect, transmit, upload, or sync any of your personal or physiological data to external servers, cloud databases, or third-party analytical engines.
* **No Persistence:** Once you close the application or clear your session, the active computational data is discarded. We use isolated local storage (`SharedPreferences`) solely to save your language preference and consent status (e.g., whether you have accepted the legal disclaimer). 

## 3. Compliance with Global Data Protection Laws
Because FuzzyGuard operates on a zero-data-collection architecture, it is inherently compliant with major global privacy frameworks, including but not limited to:
* **GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation - EU):** We practice absolute data minimization. No PII (Personally Identifiable Information) or sensitive health data is processed outside your device.
* **KVKK (Personal Data Protection Law - Turkey):** We do not act as a "Data Controller" or "Data Processor" as defined by the law, as we have no access to the user data.
* **CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act):** We do not collect, sell, or share personal information.
